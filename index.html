02 / About

From HR execution to system thinking

My journey started from HR execution, but the more I worked, the more I found myself drawn to structure: how processes are built, how systems support people, and how data can improve clarity and decisions.

How I work

I started from HR execution, working closely with performance, promotion, and day-to-day people operations. Over time, I found myself repeatedly dealing with the same type of problems: processes that relied too much on individual experience, systems that could not fully support business needs, and data that existed but was difficult to interpret or act on. This led me to gradually shift from execution to structure — focusing on how HR processes can be redesigned into clearer, more scalable, and more sustainable systems.
